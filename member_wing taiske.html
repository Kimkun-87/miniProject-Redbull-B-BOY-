About " B-BOY Taiske"

Taisuke is a typical Japanese B-boy and Red Bull BC One All-Star.
Red Bull BC One World Finals has been performed four times, but each time bad luck followed him.
In 2008, he reached the final at the BC One World Finals in Paris, but gave up the victory to Korean B-boy Wing.
