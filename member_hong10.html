About " B-BOY HONG10"

It's called B-boy Hong10.
Hong-ten. His real name is Kim Hong-yeol.
I thought it was Mr. Hong. When foreign B-boys found it difficult to pronounce
"Hong" fever, they asked them to call it "Ten = 10," but their name was cemented.
He is the second person in the world to win the Red Bull BC One World Finals (2006 and 2013),
the world's second non-boying event. The first person to win two games was B-boy Lilou.
Furthermore, Red Bull BC One in 2013 boosted Hongten's reputation as it was a tournament
that brought together all the previous champions.
